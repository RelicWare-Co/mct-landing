## Design Context

### Users
Small to medium enterprises (PYMES) and businesses in need of a consolidated, reliable provider for both financial management (accounting, tax planning) and technological advancement (web development, custom software). Their goal is finding an efficient, trusted partner for "360°" growth.

### Brand Personality
Professional, dependable, and forward-thinking. Balances the conventional trust, stability, and rigor expected from a financial consultancy with the cutting-edge innovation and fluid efficiency of a tech development firm.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme:** Clean, modern, "Premium" look. Light mode exclusively.
- **Colors:** Dominant brand color is 'Vino tinto' (`#8b1c31`) paired with crisp white (`#ffffff`), subtle off-whites (`#fafafa`), and high-contrast dark text (`#111827`, `#4b5563`).
- **Visuals:** Abstract shapes and soft glows (`--shadow-glow`) to convey abstract structure and modern fluidity, avoiding literal illustrations, cartoons, or real-life photography.
- **Typography:** `Inter` for exceptional clarity and a contemporary, balanced look.

### Design Principles
1. **Harmonize Trust & Innovation:** Achieve a balanced UI that communicates both financial rigorousness (structured layouts, clear hierarchy) and technological sophistication (smooth Framer Motion interactions, abstract visuals).
2. **Clarity Without Clutter:** Ensure spacious layouts with generous padding. Text must remain scannable and digestible to explain hybrid, complex services simply.
3. **Abstract Representation:** Rely on refined abstract shapes, typography, and iconography (Lucide React) rather than photography to unify the finance and tech offerings underneath a single coherent aesthetic.
4. **Subtle Elevation:** Use existing design tokens like the wine-red glow (`--shadow-glow`) and soft, deliberate transitions to provide a premium finish without overwhelming the user.
